Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services to meet your grooming needs, including haircuts, beard trims, shaves, and facial treatments. We also provide personalized grooming advice to ensure you leave looking your best.

How much do your services cost?

Our prices start at $20 for a basic haircut, with additional services like beard trims and hot shaves priced separately. Check out our Pricing Page for detailed information.

Do I need an appointment?

We recommend booking an appointment in advance to secure your preferred time. You can easily book online via our Booking Page.

Can I pay by card?

Yes, we accept all major credit and debit cards. We also support mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, students get a 10% discount with a valid student ID. Just show it at the time of your service.

What are your operating hours?

We are open from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.
